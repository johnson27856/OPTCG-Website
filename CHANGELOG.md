# Changelog

## [Version 1.0.0] (2026-02-27)

### Features:
- Click on a card to "lock" it! This will make the card image and card stats stick to the right side of your screen for better accessibility.
- Want to view more cards? No problem! You can resize the card viewer to see more or less cards by dragging the resizer next to the scroll bar.
- Currently supported sets include: OP-01 to OP-13, OP14-EB04, EB-01, EB-02, PRB-01, and PRB-02. More to be added soon!
- Currently supported sorting criteria include: Name, Rarity, Price, Color, and Cost. More sorting criteria to be added soon!
